📘 How to Play

In KQ Who?, your mission is to identify the attacker by filtering out innocent characters using Kusto Query Language (KQL).

Examples:

Examples:

Do you want to check the species of the target?

| where Species == "robot"
 Finds only characters who are robots
| where Hair contains "blue"
 Includes characters with blue in their hair color
| where Accessory !contains "glasses"
 Excludes anyone wearing glasses

Once you think you've found the attacker, submit:

where Name == "Attacker Name"
